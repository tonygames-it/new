Frequently Asked Questions

Your guide to the world of Ancient Quest and how to master the game.

Is the site responsive?

Yes. The layout adapts to all screen sizes, and the game canvas scales to provide a seamless experience on desktop, tablet, and mobile.

Why do I see the policy notice on the home page?

The notice appears once per session to inform you about our site policies before you play. It does not block access to the game.

How are scores saved?

High scores and leaderboard entries are stored in your browser's localStorage. This means your scores are saved on your device.

How can I reset the leaderboard?

You can clear your scores by opening your browser's developer console and running this command: `localStorage.removeItem('gf_quantum_scores')`

What are the game controls?

Use the Left/Right arrow keys or A/D to move your character. Press 'P' to pause the game and 'R' to restart. On mobile devices, simply drag your finger to move.

Does the game work offline?

Yes, once the game assets are loaded and cached by your browser, you can play it without an internet connection.

What technologies does Ancient Quest use?

The game is built with pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for maximum performance and compatibility, without relying on heavy frameworks.

What are the power-ups in the game?

You can collect special orbs for abilities like Slow-Mo, Shield, and Combo Multipliers to help you achieve a higher score.
